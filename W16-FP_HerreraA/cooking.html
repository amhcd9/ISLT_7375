Cooking Colombian Tamales

Tamales are one of the most typical dishes in Colombia (South America). Usually a tamale is served for breakfast with hot chocolate and warm bread rolls; however you can present this delicious meal for luch or dinner as well.

Although this is not an easy recipe, sure you will find it very tasty.
